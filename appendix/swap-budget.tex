\chapter{Full SWAP Budget}
\label{appendix:swap}

This appendix provides the complete four-table SWAP budget referenced in Section~\ref{subsec:swap}. All values are traceable to real component datasheets: LEON4FT (Cobham Gaisler UT699E v2.3), Kintex UltraScale+ RT (Xilinx DS985), GomSpace BP4, and CubeSat Design Specification Rev~14.1~\cite{cubesat-spec}.
