# CASCADE Abstract

CASCADE, the Onboard Crop Anomaly Screening, Confirmation, and Alert Downlink Engine, is an adaptive SmallSat payload concept for regenerative agriculture. It screens crop fields onboard, confirms likely stress with fused vegetation, thermal, and water-stress signals, and downlinks only compact geo-tagged alert tiles for scouting and targeted treatment. The key features are a Bayesian belief gate, a finite-horizon scheduler over SKIP, MOD13, FUSE, and FUSE_PRIORITY actions, a three-channel Crop Stress Composite using EVI, LST, and NDWI, and explicit power, compute, storage, and downlink constraints. In a 100-seed Monte Carlo benchmark, CASCADE reduces downlink by 99.1%, saves 38.3% energy, preserves 100% benchmark anomaly recall, and holds false positives to 0.6%. Real MODIS replays over Westlands/Firebaugh separate a 2014 D4 drought season from a quiet 2024 season without retuning.
