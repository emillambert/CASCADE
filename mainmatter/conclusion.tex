\section{Conclusion}

\label{chapter:conclusion}

MASFE demonstrates that adaptive sensing and onboard processing can transform SmallSat missions from passive collectors into responsive decision systems. The two-stage MDP scheduling policy selects, schedules, and fuses NASA MOD13A1 and MOD11A1 algorithms in real time based on scene content rather than a fixed plan — achieving 97.8\% downlink reduction, sub-90-minute alert latency, and 100\% disease-event recall while staying within hosted-payload SWAP constraints. For regenerative agriculture, this means the difference between blanket preventative treatment and targeted, evidence-based intervention at field-patch scale. The orchestration engine is the contribution; agriculture is its strongest first proof point.
