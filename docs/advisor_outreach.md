## Two-sentence project summary

MASFE is a hosted-payload concept that runs a deterministic MDP-style policy onboard a LEON4FT-class processor to decide when to trigger thermal confirmation and priority downlink for crop-stress alerts. The current repo includes a 100-seed Monte Carlo benchmark, a real MODIS replay over Westlands/Firebaugh, and a five-year pilot-to-scale business model.
