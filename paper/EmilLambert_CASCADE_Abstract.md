# CASCADE Abstract

CASCADE---An Onboard Crop Anomaly Screening, Confirmation, and Alert Downlink Engine---is a SmallSat adaptive-sensing concept for regenerative agriculture. Crop stress is the reference application here, but the same staged onboard triage architecture can be retuned for adjacent land-resilience tasks. CASCADE orchestrates MOD13A1-style EVI screening, MOD11A1-style thermal confirmation, and MOD09A1-derived NDWI fusion under explicit power, compute, storage, and downlink limits. In a 100-seed Monte Carlo benchmark it achieves 99.1% downlink reduction versus raw, 38.3% energy savings, 100% benchmark anomaly-event recall, and a 0.6% false-positive rate while priority-downlinking only geo-tagged alert tiles for grower scouting and targeted treatment. A 2024 Westlands/Firebaugh MODIS replay exercises the policy on real scenes; the critical path to TRL 4 is hardware-in-the-loop validation.
